## Evidence Status Labels

Confirmed:
Reviewed material directly supports the content.

Partially Confirmed:
Reviewed material supports related capability, but exact name, ownership, or detail needs confirmation.

Needs Confirmation:
User asked to include it, but reviewed materials did not contain enough supporting evidence.

## Content Style

Use:

- clear business problem
- role ownership
- architecture detail
- product and UX thinking
- reusable value
- measurable outcomes when confirmed
- precise Appian terminology

Avoid:

- generic "worked on development" wording
- unverified numbers
- internal object IDs
- raw export details
- secret-like data
- template language
