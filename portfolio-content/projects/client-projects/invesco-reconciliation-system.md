## Hero Summary

Appian reconciliation automation for investment operations, using SFTP batch ingestion, Excel/CSV processing, optimized SQL, dashboards, and smart-service based workflow automation.

## Project Context

The system was designed for an investment management environment to compare and verify two sets of data, identify differences, support investigation, reconcile records, and report outcomes.

## Problem

Reconciliation processes can become manual, repetitive, and error-prone when data arrives from external systems or files. Teams need automation for data intake, comparison, exception handling, investigation, and reporting.
