## Hero Summary

Reusable Appian date and date-time input component designed for locale-sensitive enterprise applications requiring consistent regional formatting, accessibility, and user-friendly data entry.

## Project Context

Enterprise Appian applications may serve users across regions, business units, and date/time conventions. Standard date inputs may not always meet localized formatting expectations, validation rules, or advanced enterprise UX requirements.

This project should be positioned as a client plugin development effort once source evidence, screenshots, or implementation details are added.

## Problem

Users in different locales interpret dates differently. A date such as `03/04/2026` can mean March 4 or April 3 depending on region. Enterprise forms need clear, locale-aware date and date-time capture to avoid data-entry errors, process issues, and downstream reporting problems.
