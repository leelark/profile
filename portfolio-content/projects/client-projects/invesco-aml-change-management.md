## Hero Summary

Appian-based Change Management and Anti-Money Laundering workflow support for an investment management environment, covering architecture participation, database/process design, and application module development.

## Project Context

The project supported an investment management company with Change Management and Anti-Money Laundering workflows.

Change management helps identify the need for change, assess impact, communicate updates, and manage resistance or challenges.

AML workflows support customer identification, verification, transaction monitoring, and suspicious activity reporting.

## Problem

Financial services organizations need controlled, auditable workflows for change management and AML processes. These workflows require structured data, approvals, monitoring, reporting, and compliance support.
