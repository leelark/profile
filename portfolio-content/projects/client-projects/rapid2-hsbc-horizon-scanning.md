## Hero Summary

Appian regulatory horizon-scanning platform for ingesting, triaging, and handling regulatory alerts across developments, consultations, fines, enforcements, and news.

## Project Context

RAPID2 is HSBC's system for ingesting, triaging, and handling alerts from global regulatory bodies. The platform helps support efficient and timely responses to compliance issues as they arise.

Alerts are triaged into categories such as:

- regulatory developments
- consultations
- fines and enforcements
- news

## Problem

Regulatory teams need to track global regulatory events, categorize them, route them, and respond on time. Manual tracking creates risk around missed deadlines, inconsistent triage, and limited auditability.
