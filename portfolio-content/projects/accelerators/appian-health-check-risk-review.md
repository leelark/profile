## Hero Summary

Health Check review accelerator that converts automated Appian risk findings into a prioritized remediation backlog for design, performance, integration, interface, process, and sizing concerns.

## Project Context

Appian Health Check output can contain many findings across applications, design objects, queries, integrations, process models, and interfaces. Delivery teams need help separating outliers from meaningful risks and translating findings into action.

## Problem

The reviewed material showed the need to:

- interpret automated Health Check findings
- prioritize high-impact risks
- validate findings with delivery teams
- separate outliers from patterns
- review slow queries, interfaces, integrations, and process models
- connect risk findings to remediation actions
- include process sizing considerations
