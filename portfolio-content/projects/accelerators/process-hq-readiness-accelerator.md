## Hero Summary

Process intelligence readiness accelerator for enabling business users to explore Data Fabric and Process Insights through governed datasets, secure access, case/event modeling, and data preparation.

## Project Context

Business teams want to understand process bottlenecks and operational performance without relying on developers or external data-science workflows for every insight. Process HQ and Data Fabric Insights can support this, but readiness depends on data model, sync behavior, security, governance, and process history structure.

## Problem

Teams needed guidance on:

- what Process HQ provides
- how Data Fabric Insights and Process Insights differ
- which record types and datasets are required
- what security and role setup is needed
- how case and event history should be modeled
- how data stewards prepare and transform data
- how business users can move from insight to action
