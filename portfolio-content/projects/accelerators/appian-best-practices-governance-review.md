## Hero Summary

Customer-facing Appian governance review checklist covering process models, SAIL, interfaces, integrations, data, security, records, scalability, and application-wide design practices.

## Project Context

Enterprise Appian teams need repeatable quality reviews that are clear enough for clients, detailed enough for technical teams, and structured enough to drive remediation ownership.

## Problem

Without a consistent checklist, reviews can become:

- subjective
- difficult to compare across applications
- disconnected from remediation ownership
- hard to explain to clients
- inconsistent across reviewers
- weak on evidence and risk levels
