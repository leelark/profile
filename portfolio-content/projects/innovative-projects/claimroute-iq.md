## Hero Summary

AI-powered claims intelligence platform for insurance claim intake, document classification, medical data extraction, eligibility validation, reviewer routing, support chatbot, and full claim route logging.

## Project Context

ClaimRoute IQ is an Appian-based claims automation product designed to streamline insurance claim submission and review. It combines user portals, OTP login, document upload, AI extraction, eligibility checks, manual review tasks, reviewer dashboards, support chatbot capability, and route logs.

## Problem

Insurance claims are document-heavy and decision-heavy. A claim reviewer may need to inspect medical reports, invoices, patient details, treatment coverage, disease coverage, balance limits, fraud indicators, and policy rules before making a decision.

Manual processing creates:

- slow turnaround
- inconsistent eligibility checks
- high reviewer workload
- limited transparency
- repeated support questions
- difficulty routing exceptions

## Solution Overview

ClaimRoute IQ automates the claim journey from submission to decision:

1. User login through OTP.
2. Claim request submission.
3. Document upload.
4. Document classification.
5. Medical report extraction.
6. Invoice extraction.
7. Eligibility checks.
8. Claim amount validation.
9. AI-based decision recommendation.
10. Manual reviewer routing where needed.
11. Claim route logging.
12. Support chatbot response.
13. Reviewer dashboard and task management.
