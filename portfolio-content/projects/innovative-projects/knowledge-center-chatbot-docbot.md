## Hero Summary

Appian-native document intelligence assistant that turns PDFs into grounded answers, retrieves page-level evidence, and highlights exact source passages through a custom document viewer.

## Project Context

Knowledge Center Chatbot, also positioned as DocBot, is a customizable document AI chatbot built on Appian. It helps users upload, train, query, and explore documents through natural language.

The product combines document indexing, Appian AI, search, page-level evidence retrieval, BBox metadata, and a custom HTML document viewer.

## Problem

Enterprise teams need accurate answers from policies, contracts, reports, technical documents, and regulatory material. Generic chatbots may hallucinate or fail to show the source of an answer.

Users need:

- grounded answers
- exact source evidence
- document search
- page-level references
- reduced manual reading
- confidence in AI responses
