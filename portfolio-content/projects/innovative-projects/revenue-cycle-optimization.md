## Hero Summary

Healthcare revenue-cycle workflow automation for referrals, pre-authorization, eligibility and benefits verification, denial management, reporting, and smart-service driven data operations.

## Project Context

Revenue cycle optimization supports healthcare facilities and clinicians by centralizing and automating operational workflows such as referrals, pre-authorization, eligibility checks, benefits verification, denial management, and reporting.

## Problem

Healthcare revenue-cycle workflows involve multiple manual steps, payer interactions, document checks, and reporting needs. Delays or errors can affect reimbursement, billing accuracy, denial rates, and operational efficiency.
