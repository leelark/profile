## Hero Summary

Appian traceability workflow for manufacturing and assembly data capture, analytics dashboards, barcode/simulator integration, and document evidence highlighting.

## Project Context

The project focused on automated capture, storage, management, and analysis of data collected from manufacturing and assembly systems across production stages.

The old portfolio describes the architecture as influenced by Industrial IoT and data analytics, enabling real-time and error-free capture from PLC-style sources.

## Problem

Manufacturing teams need traceability across production stages. Manual or disconnected data capture can reduce visibility, increase quality risk, and make it harder to investigate production issues.
