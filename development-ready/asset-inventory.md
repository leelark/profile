## Sanitization Rules

Before an asset is public:

- remove client names unless approved
- remove usernames
- remove email addresses
- remove internal URLs
- remove environment names
- remove Appian object IDs
- remove exact metrics unless approved
- remove secrets or token-like values

## Launch Blockers

These must be resolved before production launch:

- professional headshot
- resume PDF or decision to omit resume download
- production Open Graph image
- LinkedIn URL
- contact form production test
- approved screenshots or sanitized replacement visuals
